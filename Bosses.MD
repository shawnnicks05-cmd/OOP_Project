# LEVEL 1 BOSS — HYDRA

## Hydra of Academics
**Role:** Multi-Headed Academic Monster  
**Difficulty:** Easy-Medium  
**Damage Type:** Mental / Psychological (Stress-inducing damage that bypasses physical armor)  
**Boss Meter:** **[ RAGE METER ]** *(Starts at 0% ──> Fills to 100%)*  
*   *Mechanic:* When the Rage Meter crosses 80%, the Hydra radiates immense stress, inflicting continuous **Damage over Time (DoT)** to all active students.

---

## Hydra Heads

### Oral Recitation Head
- Causes panic effects.
- Can silence players.
- Weak against confidence buffs.

### Quiz Head
- Fast attacks.
- Surprise damage.
- Weak against analysis skills.

### Activity Head
- Continuous pressure damage.
- Spawns mini tasks.

### Exam Head
- Massive boss damage.
- Applies stress debuff.
- Final-phase enemy.

### Project Offense Head
- Long-term pressure attacks.
- Requires teamwork to defeat.

### Skills
- **Oral Barrage**
  - Rapid oral recitation attacks.

- **Pop Quiz**
  - Random surprise damage to all students.

- **Deadline Slam**
  - Applies stress debuff.

- **Exam Phase**
  - Increases all Hydra damage by 25%.

### Passive
- **Multiple Heads**
  - Can attack multiple players at once.
