# Character Statistics and Damage System Manual

This document provides a comprehensive structural breakdown of the attribute types, damage classification mechanics, status effects, and character roles found within the **STUDENTvsFACULTY** system.

---

## 1. Primary Character Attributes (Best Stats)

Every Core Team student character possesses a primary attribute optimized for their strategic utility and damage output. These "Best Stats" represent the mechanical scaling factors for their combat kits.

### 📊 Intelligence
* **Primary User:** Shawn ("The Strategist")
* **Mechanical Profile:** Governs critical thinking, structural team buffs, tactical coordination, and Area-of-Effect (AOE) crowd control. It scales multi-target abilities and amplifies team efficiency.
* **Thematic Definition:** The capacity for high-level tactical planning and logistical execution under pressure.

### 📈 Analysis
* **Primary User:** Ethan ("Data Analyst")
* **Mechanical Profile:** Dictates vulnerability identification, critical strike probability, accuracy parameters, and single-target high-precision execution. 
* **Thematic Definition:** The ability to process data patterns and isolate operational flaws within opposing targets.

### 🎨 Creativity
* **Primary User:** Dwight ("UI Specialist")
* **Mechanical Profile:** Drives rapid deployment mechanics, technological modifications, temporary shield health values, and status disruption (Confusion).
* **Thematic Definition:** Out-of-the-box system configurations that subvert standard operational limits to protect and advance team assets.

### 🔮 Wisdom
* **Primary User:** Omar ("Human ChatGPT")
* **Mechanical Profile:** Facilitates adaptive capability replication, knowledge-based spellcasting loops, rapid responsive action triggers, and stamina/focus restoration dynamics.
* **Thematic Definition:** Deeply integrated conceptual comprehension allowing for instantaneous, flexible execution.

### 📖 Knowledge
* **Primary User:** Princess ("The Source")
* **Mechanical Profile:** Determines health/mana restoration throughput, anti-misinformation/debuff resistance capabilities, and educational stat-boosting support actions.
* **Thematic Definition:** Access to verified, absolute institutional information used as a fundamental protective resource.

## 3. Secondary Combat & Resource Gauges

Beyond primary statistics, combat loops are dictated by four functional pools governing skill uptime and survival:

* **HP (Health Points):** Total structural durability before a character falls unconscious. Restored primarily via Knowledge-based support actions.
* **Mana:** The energy currency consumed to execute unique Skills.
* **Stamina:** A rate-limiting gauge governing actions per turn. Depleted by complex combat actions and heavy physical faculty debuffs; restored by focus-oriented actions.
* **Focus / Morale:** A psychological performance modifier. High focus optimizes accuracy and critical parameters; low focus triggers standard combat performance drops.

## 4. Status Effects & Mechanical Conditions

Faculty boss actions alter student efficiency by applying specific combat debuffs. The core kit is designed to mitigate or entirely counter these states:

### ⚠️ Panic
* **Source Examples:** Oral Recitation Head, Terror Recitation, Surprise Practical Exam.
* **Mechanical Effect:** Restricts ability accuracy, causes irregular turn sequencing, or triggers self-inflicted action failure rates.
* **Countermeasures:** Shawn's *Deadline Focus*, Princess's *Verified Facts*.

### 📉 Stress / Burnout
* **Source Examples:** Exam Head, Deadline Slam, Impossible Deadline, Final Defense.
* **Mechanical Effect:** Gradual resource depletion (Stamina/HP) over time and heavily reduced action speeds.
* **Countermeasures:** Team Combo: *Finals Week Survival*, Shawn's *Deadline Focus*.

### 🔇 Silence
* **Source Examples:** Oral Recitation Head, Knowledge Suppression, Final Defense.
* **Mechanical Effect:** Complete lockout of mana-consuming active Skills, restricting characters to basic commands.
* **Countermeasures:** Princess's *Citation Heal* (Purge variants), Team Combo: *Finals Week Survival*.

### 🌀 Confusion
* **Source Examples:** UI Overload, Confusing Question, Information Overload.
* **Mechanical Effect:** Randomizes targeted actions, causing characters to potentially target allies or waste turns.
* **Countermeasures:** Princess's *Verified Facts*.
